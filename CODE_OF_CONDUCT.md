# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in Inowio D2d a respectful, inclusive, and productive experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, discussions, and code reviews.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for the impact of your words and actions.
- Keep feedback focused on the project, the code, and the user experience.
- Welcome contributors with different skill levels, backgrounds, and perspectives.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or sustained disruption of project discussions.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwanted attention.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make participation unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, temporarily limit participation, or permanently ban contributors who violate this code of conduct.

To report unacceptable behavior, email inowio@outlook.com. Reports will be reviewed privately and handled as fairly and promptly as possible.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including GitHub repositories, issue trackers, discussions, pull requests, release channels, and any public communication made on behalf of the project.
